## More Markdown for Math Formulas

Common formulas for perimeter, circumference, area, and volume.

**Perimeter**
$$\begin{align}
Square: P &= 4a \\
Rectangle: P &= 2(l+w)
\end{align}$$

**Circumference**
$$\begin{align} 
Circle: C = 2 \pi r 
\end{align}$$

**Area**
$$\begin{align}
Square: A &= a^2 \\
Rectangle: A &= lw \\
Triangle: A &= \frac{bh}{2} \\
Trapezoid: A &= \frac{(b^1 + b^2)h}{2} \\
Circle: A &= \pi r^2
\end{align}$$

**Surface Area**
$$\begin{align}
Cube: SA &= 6a^2 \\
Cylinder: SA &= 2\pi r(r+h) \\
Cone: SA &= \pi r^2 + \pi rl \\
Sphere: SA &= 4 \pi r^2
\end{align}$$

**Volume**
$$\begin{align}
Cube: V &= a^3 \\
Cylinder: V &= \pi r^2h \\
Cone: V &= \frac{\pi r^2 h}{3} \\
Sphere: V &= \frac{4\pi r^3}{3}
\end{align}$$
